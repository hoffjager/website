Famous Quotes

Cynicism

A cynic is a man who knows the price of everything,
but the value of nothing.
Oscar Wilde

Oscar Wilde (1854 - 1900) was an Irish writer and poet.

Happiness

The secret of happiness is not in doing what one likes,
but in liking what one has to do.
James M. Barrie

James M. Barrie (1860 - 1937) was a Scottish author and dramatist.
